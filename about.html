Well, I'm a 23-year-old guy based in Negombo, Sri Lanka. I've been doing amateur edits on Photoshop since I was 10 years old. Since then, I've learned, practised, and updated myself to be better at it.

At the age of 15, I learnt to edit videos on the spot thanks to a 20-minute tutorial just because there was footage of the college athletic meet with no one to edit a film out of it. My interest in video editing and videography was ignited by that, and I eventually followed that path.

I was hired by a marketing start-up to handle all areas of videography (planning, production, and post-production) for them and their clients as soon as I completed my Advanced Level exams. Later, I became a part of the Shift Dimensions creative agency, where I am in charge of videography, editing, brand management, strategy, Meta Ads (formerly known as the Facebook family of applications), and new clients for the business.

I enrolled in the Creative Advertising HND program at AMDT: School of Creativity in 2020, intending to expand my knowledge and experience. Sept. 2022 is the projected completion date for the HND.
