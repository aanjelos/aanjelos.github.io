CHILLI PASTE
(MASTER BLEND/VEGAN)

Have it with the fried rice you're eating, or mix it to that delicious dish you're making; Chilli Magic's Chilli paste will surely take your tastebuds to a better experience.
Made with; chilli flakes ground from hand-cleaned and dried red chillies and dried baby shrimp to elevate that perfect taste (The same quality but without the shrimp if you're vegan!) And a generous amount of vegetable oil for the ideal texture.
Available in 100g and 200g bottles. Customized orders can also be done on request.

MALDIVE FISH SAMBOL
(Wïn,lv iïfndA,)

The main ingredient of the Maldive fish sambol is, as the name suggests, Maldive Fish. Cleaned, smoked, and sun-dried tuna with a hard texture. We, Sri Lankans, use them in small portions when cooking curries and dishes but, it's optional. But with the sambol, we're using cups of Maldive fish instead of a tablespoon.
Mixed with onions, chillies, curry leaves, and more; this crunchy, sweet and salty, flavourful sambol is a perfect addition to have with your rice dishes and more. Especially with a biriyani.
Available in 100g bottles only.
