"Terra" is a second-generation attempt of promoting eco-friendly & artisanally crafted home & garden products.

As a full-time law student and a part-time pot dealer to be (bear with me, I couldn't resist the pun!) "Terra" is my way of continuing what my parents have built from the ground up with years of relentless perseverance. The goal is to continue enriching the collective of artisanal craftsmen throughout the country who has joined hands with "Terra" over the years.
