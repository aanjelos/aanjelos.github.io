Frequently Asked Questions

  • What happens after I sign up?

    We will contact you to know more about your business information. Then we will work together on creating a unique landing page for your business which can feature your products, pricing info, location, links to your social media etc. Your website can be updated later on your request. All the ads will be run by us, Ceylon Directory on Facebook, Instagram, and Google; using content from all our vendors.

  • What am I paying for?

    Your registration fee will be mainly going towards setting up your unique landing page which is designed by our skilled web designers. The monthly fee is used towards running Ceylon Directory ads and site maintainance.

  • Can I update my website information later?

    Yes! You can change pricing or update your products once a month with no extra fees! It's all included in the subscription.

  • How will the ads make an impact?

    The ads will be made with all our vendors in mind. It could be ads combining a few vendors or an ad dedicated specially for one. But all our vendors have one thing in common. Which is, the demography. Almost all of our vendors will be aiming for people which tend to make purchases online and support small businesses. These are the audiences which we will be targetting on our Facebook, Instagram and Google ads.

  • How long will I stay on the "coming soon" stage?

    Our new vendors' entry will be added to our page within 24 hours of signing up. But their landing page will require some time to be set up. But this will be completed and your page will be up and running in less than 14 days.

  • How to cancel my subscription?

    Contact our team and let us know. You won't be charged next month, and your entry will be removed from our site. But we do keep your landing page in our backups for 28 days if you decide to join us back with no registration fee.
