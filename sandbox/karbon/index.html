Who We Are

Karbon International began export operations in the year 2022 with Cinnamon quills and other Cinnamon products such as Cinnamon Bark Oil and Cinnamon Leaf Oil. We have expanded our offerings to include Black Pepper, Nutmeg and Turmeric and more.

Our Spices are sourced from local farming communities, then processed and packaged for export, we ensure products are grown and processed to strict quality standards. At the core of our supply chain are our farmers who are well-experienced and have been growing spices over generations, the best quality produce is then brought in for processing and packaging according to customer requirements before being exported via our logistics partners.

What We Do

Our products can be supplied in bulk form for processing and final packaging by our customers. We are also able to provide retail packaged products according to our customers branding requirements if it is deemed efficient and cost effective.

Certification is done according to customer requirements, and we also provide an Organic range of products. Our certifications can include FSSC22000 and a validated kill-step for pathogens, we are able to use steam treatment and dry heat to treat various types of spices.
