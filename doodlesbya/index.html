HOW TO ORDER

Send your order request through WhatsApp or through the web form below mentioning the product code and the quantity you wish to order. Along with your name, phone number, email, and delivery address.

 

I will get back to you ASAP after checking the availability of the stock.

 

Afterwards; proceed with the payment (details mentioned below), and send the payment slip through WhatsApp or email to hello@doodlesbya.com

 

Your order will be handed over to the post office the next Monday and will be sent to you through registered post.

 

Please note that the posters are printed after order confirmation.

Currently, Only bank deposits and online transfers are supported.

ABOUT ME

Oh, hello there!

I started 'Doodles By A' as a fun side project after trying out some mandala art and really liking them. For these, I use a Huion HS610 pen tablet along with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.
